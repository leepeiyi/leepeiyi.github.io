Parkify

About: Streamlining your parking experience with our project, simplifying your decision-making process by instantly providing the top 10 nearest parking options along with rates. Powered by microservices and deployed with Docker for seamless accessibility

My Contribution: I utilized the Google Geocode API to obtain the geocode of the user's specified destination. Using a distance formula, I determined the ten nearest carparks to the user's IP address. With the geocode and calculated distances, I then correlated these carparks with available data from the URA API. Finally, I helped to display the top ten carparks on a map

Prototypes

Pharmalicious

About: This app is designed specifically to assist users in remembering to take their medication, particularly in households with elderly members who may forget. It enables family members to remind each other about medication schedules and also serves as a platform for monitoring medication stock levels at home, preventing over-purchasing. Through this project, I gained valuable insights into the prototyping process, learning various heuristics and techniques to design user-friendly prototypes.

NVPC

About: For this project, we collaborated with NVPC (National Volunteer & Philanthropy Centre) as our real client. Our main objective was to devise a solution to incentivize startups to engage in volunteering, particularly in their early stages when resources are limited. We proposed enhancing their existing but underutilized application to serve as a platform for communication among different participant groups, facilitating project discussions and interactions. Our solution involved enabling startups to form groups and initiate activities where they could share existing resources. We developed a prototype demonstrating how startups could connect, identify common interests, and collaborate on volunteering initiatives.

Anytime Fitness

About: For this project, our focus was on selecting an existing company and evaluating the necessity for digital transformation, along with proposing strategies to facilitate this transformation. During our analysis, a significant issue emerged when a gym member was falsely accused of tailgating, sparking controversy. To address this, we proposed integrating IoT sensors at the entrance and within the app to notify users of tailgating incidents, enabling staff to resolve such issues immediately. Additionally, we explored solutions to enhance the overall customer experience, including IoT sensors on equipment for better tracking and member satisfaction, as well as offering virtual coaching via the app for convenient workouts anytime, anywhere.

AirBnB

About: In this project, we conducted an investment analysis focusing on Airbnb rentals to determine the timeframe for investors to achieve profitability.
